Skoo is a collaboration of enthusiast of different backgrounds covering, software development, hardware design, transportation logistics and retail management.

Skoo EVE

EVE is a device that can be installed on any motor vehicle worldwide. The device enhances security of you vehicle or motor bike, and allows you to become an owner of an automated rental, which can bring you return on your investment or extra cash when you need it, depending on how often you’d like to utilize your vehicle as a part of Skoo’s global rental fleet.

DEMO
  1. Location Data - Eve is a sophisticated combination of hardware to determine a precise location of your Vehicle throught AWS.
  2. Security - Skoo security token is a low powered device that will make sure your Vehicle will never be lost and send a location signal to the server upon request even all the batteries of your vehicle are out of service
  3. Customizaion - Skoo can customize Skoo EVE design to be flexible and easily install anywhere inside of your vehicle or motorbike 

Skoo App

The Skoo APP, through GPS enabled EVE attached to your vehicle or motobike will enable registered riders to find, unlock, and ride it. Besides earning revenue, the APP takes the hassle out of this transaction by collecting necessary pre-authorizations, liability waivers, and rider’s billing info, all together making rental process easier and more secure.
